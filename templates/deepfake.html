DeepFake Detection

Deepfake technology leverages artificial intelligence to produce convincing but fabricated media content, often used to create deceptive videos. It raises significant concerns about the spread of misinformation and poses serious threats to individual privacy and security due to its ability to generate realistic yet entirely fake portrayals of people.

  • Misinformation: Alters videos to spread false narratives.
  • Privacy: Raises concerns by creating fabricated personal videos.

Strategies to Protect Yourself from Deepfakes

  • Verify media sources and authenticity before sharing or believing.
  • Avoid sharing personal or sensitive information online.
  • Use reputable antivirus software and keep it updated.
  • Be cautious of unexpected or suspicious requests for information or actions.
  • Stay informed about evolving deepfake detection technologies and strategies.
